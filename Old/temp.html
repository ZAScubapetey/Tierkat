About Us

Tierkat boxes are locally produced and made from earth-friendly farmed Kiaat which is commonly found in central and southern Africa and is well- known in South Africa for its beautiful colour and durability.

It is also known as “mukwa” or “bloedhout”, so named after its unique colouring. Most of the wood is dark brown, but there are flashes of lightly coloured sapwood and beautiful red veins that provide an attractive contrast.

As a result of our passion for D&D, we crafted a range of products to empower you on your next dungeon quest. Our products a way to spoil yourself. Or the perfect gift for that nerdy friend.

At Tierkat.co.za we live and breathe gaming and we look forward to sharing that passion with you.
