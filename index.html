About Us

Tierkat boxes are locally produced and made from earth-friendly farmed Kiaat which is commonly found in central and southern Africa and is well- known in South Africa for its beautiful colour and durability.
It is also known as “mukwa” or “bloedhout”, so named after its unique colouring. Most of the wood is dark brown, but there are flashes of lightly coloured sapwood and beautiful red veins that provide an attractive contrast.

The Yellowwood is one of South Africa’s most valued trees. It is also South Africa’s National Tree. It grows naturally in mountainous areas and forests in the southern, eastern and northern parts of South Africa, extending into Zimbabwe and further north. The soft yellow colour of the wood contrasts beautifully with the colors of the felt inserts as it polishes up very well.

Manufacturing and delivery of your new dice box may take a few days as we machine, laser engrave and polish each box lovingly as per order, each one unique and custom.
We use teak oil which is a deep penetrating mineral oil specially formulated to protect and enrich natural solid timbers. These oils prevent the boxes from drying and cracking.
If needed you may touch up faded boxes with normal furniture oil.

As a result of our passion for D&D, we crafted a range of products to empower you on your next dungeon quest. Our products is an elegant way to spoil yourself. Or the perfect gift for that nerdy tabletop gaming friend.

At Tierkat.co.za we live and breathe gaming and we look forward to sharing that passion with you.
